1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, such as your preferred language and other settings. This can make your next visit easier and the site more useful to you.

2. How We Use Cookies

In Safari Match, we use cookies for several important purposes:

  • Essential Functionality: Cookies that are necessary for the game to function properly
  • Performance Monitoring: Understanding how the game performs and identifying issues
  • User Experience: Remembering your preferences and settings
  • Analytics: Learning how players interact with the game to improve it
  • Security: Protecting against fraud and ensuring account safety

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for Safari Match to function and cannot be disabled:

  • Session Cookies: Maintain your game session and prevent data loss
  • Authentication Cookies: Keep you logged in and secure
  • Security Cookies: Protect against malicious activities and fraud
  • Load Balancing Cookies: Ensure smooth game performance

3.2 Performance Cookies

These cookies help us understand how Safari Match performs:

  • Analytics Cookies: Track game usage, popular features, and player behavior
  • Error Monitoring: Identify and fix technical issues quickly
  • Performance Metrics: Measure loading times and game responsiveness
  • User Journey Tracking: Understand how players progress through the game

3.3 Preference Cookies

These cookies remember your choices and settings:

  • Language Preferences: Remember your preferred language
  • Game Settings: Store your audio, visual, and control preferences
  • Cookie Consent: Remember that you've accepted our cookie policy
  • Personalization: Customize the game experience based on your preferences

3.4 Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by third-party services we use:

  • Game Analytics: Unity Analytics, Google Analytics, and similar services
  • Social Media: Facebook, Twitter, and other social platform integrations
  • Advertising: Ad serving and measurement services (if applicable)
  • Customer Support: Help desk and support platform cookies

5. How Long Cookies Are Stored

Different types of cookies are stored for different periods:

  • Session Cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Temporary Cookies: Stored for a few hours or days
  • Persistent Cookies: Stored for months or years
  • Third-Party Cookies: Duration determined by the third-party service

6. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have several options for managing cookies in Safari Match:

6.1 Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Cookies and site data

6.2 Game Settings

Within Safari Match, you can control certain cookie preferences:

  • Access the game's Settings menu
  • Navigate to Privacy or Cookie settings
  • Toggle specific cookie categories on or off
  • Save your preferences

7. Impact of Disabling Cookies

While you can disable cookies, doing so may affect your Safari Match experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Disabling these will prevent the game from working properly
  • Performance Cookies: We won't be able to identify and fix technical issues quickly
  • Preference Cookies: Your settings and preferences won't be remembered
  • Analytics Cookies: We won't be able to improve the game based on usage data

8. Cookie Consent

We are committed to transparency about our cookie usage:

  • We show a clear cookie consent banner when you first visit
  • You can change your cookie preferences at any time
  • We only use cookies for the purposes described in this policy
  • We respect your choices and preferences
