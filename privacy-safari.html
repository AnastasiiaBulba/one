Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2025

Important: This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your personal information. By using Safari Match, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Safari Match, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and phone number (if provided through contact forms)
  • Game Data: Progress, scores, achievements, and gameplay statistics
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser version, and IP address
  • Usage Analytics: How you interact with the game, features used, and time spent playing
  • Technical Data: Error logs, performance metrics, and crash reports

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing and improving the Safari Match gaming experience
  • Personalizing game content and recommendations
  • Analyzing game performance and identifying areas for improvement
  • Providing customer support and responding to inquiries
  • Sending important updates and notifications about the game
  • Ensuring game security and preventing fraud
  • Complying with legal obligations and regulations

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party services that help us operate the game (hosting, analytics, customer support)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • Consent: When you explicitly give us permission to share your information

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls and authentication measures
  • Employee training on data protection practices
  • Incident response procedures for data breaches

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide the game service and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy:

  • Game Data: Retained while your account is active and for a reasonable period after deactivation
  • Contact Information: Retained for customer support purposes and as required by law
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated and anonymized data may be retained for research purposes
  • Legal Requirements: Some information may be retained longer if required by law

6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service
  • Objection: Object to certain types of data processing
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent for data processing where applicable

Safari Match uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your gaming experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic game functionality

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences, though disabling certain cookies may affect game functionality.
