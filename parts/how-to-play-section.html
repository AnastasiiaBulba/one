How to Play

Basic Rules

  • Match 3 or more identical animal blocks in a row or column
  • Create special combinations for powerful effects
  • Clear objectives to complete each level
  • Use boosters strategically to overcome obstacles

Controls

  • Mouse/Touch: Click or tap to select and swap blocks
  • Keyboard: Use arrow keys for navigation
  • Mobile: Swipe to move blocks

Tips & Tricks

  • Look for potential matches before making moves
  • Save boosters for difficult situations
  • Focus on completing objectives first
  • Plan your moves ahead to create combos
